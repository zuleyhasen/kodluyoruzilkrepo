Annabelle

IMDb RATING: 5.5/10

  •    "Annabelle" is a spin-off from The Conjuring universe, focusing on a cursed doll that wreaks havoc wherever it goes. The first film, Annabelle (2014), shows how the doll becomes linked to dark supernatural forces after a cult ritual. With its eerie atmosphere, the film explores themes of possession, loss, and terror. Its sequels, Annabelle: Creation (2017) and Annabelle Comes Home (2019), delve into the doll’s origins and its impact on different families and individuals. This series is renowned for its tension-filled scenes and creepy settings, solidifying its place as a modern horror favorite.

IT

IMDb RATING: 7.3/10

  •    Based on Stephen King’s novel, It is a two-part film series (It (2017) and It Chapter Two (2019)) that tells the story of a group of children, "The Losers' Club," who face off against Pennywise, a shape-shifting entity that takes the form of a terrifying clown. The first film is set in the 1980s and focuses on childhood fears, while the sequel explores how those fears linger into adulthood. Known for its gripping storytelling, psychological depth, and spine-chilling visuals, It combines supernatural horror with emotional narratives about friendship and overcoming trauma. Both films stand as iconic examples of contemporary horror, offering scares alongside compelling character development.
